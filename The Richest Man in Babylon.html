The Richest Man in Babylon

Author: George S. Clason

Summary: The Richest Man in Babylon by George S. Clason is a timeless guide to financial wisdom, presented through parables set in the ancient city of Babylon. The book shares practical lessons on wealth-building, saving, and financial management through the stories of characters like Arkad, the titular "richest man in Babylon."

Key principles include saving at least 10% of your income, investing wisely, avoiding debt, and seeking advice from knowledgeable individuals. Clason emphasizes the importance of discipline, financial planning, and making money work for you to achieve prosperity.

Written in a simple yet engaging style, the book offers timeless advice on building wealth and living a financially stable life.

Published: 1926
